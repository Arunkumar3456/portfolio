PROJECT 2

“Embedded-Based Auto Cart and Android IoT App for Social Distancing Shopping”

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The "Embedded-Based Auto Cart and Android IoT App for Social Distancing Shopping" project aims to develop an automated shopping cart equipped with sensors, navigation systems, and an Android IoT app. The cart autonomously follows customers and avoids obstacles while the app enhances the shopping experience by providing features like item tracking and contactless payments. This integration ensures a safe and efficient shopping experience while maintaining social distancing guidelines.
