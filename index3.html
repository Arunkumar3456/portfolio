PROJECT 2

“Embedded-Based Auto Cart and Android IoT App for Social Distancing Shopping”

PROJECT OVERVIEW

“Embedded-Based Auto Cart and Android IoT App for Social Distancing Shopping” for easy shopping” This project aims to develop an embedded-based auto cart and an Android IoT app to facilitate easy and safe shopping while adhering to social distancing guidelines. The auto cart is equipped with sensors and navigation systems to autonomously follow customers and avoid obstacles. The Android IoT app enhances the shopping experience by allowing customers to locate items, track their cart, and make contactless payments. Together, these technologies ensure a seamless, efficient, and socially distanced shopping experience.
